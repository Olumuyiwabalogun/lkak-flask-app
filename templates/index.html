About LKAK Limited

LKAK LIMITED is a multidisciplinary consulting and contracting firm with deep-rooted experience across agriculture, livestock, agro-industry, value chain development, monitoring & evaluation, climate resilience, irrigation, water resources, infrastructure and development planning.

LKAK LIMITED is a multidisciplinary consulting and contracting firm offering a wide range of technical expertise in Agriculture and Food Security, Livestock Services, Agro-Industry and Agribusiness, Value Chain Development, Women and Youth Empowerment, Environment and Climate Change, Monitoring and Evaluation, Irrigation, Water Resources, Infrastructure, and Development Planning. Over the years, LKAK has supported state and federal governments, donor agencies, and private sector actors in designing, implementing, and supervising development programs across Nigeria and West Africa.

Capabilities & Qualifications

The firm brings together a multidisciplinary team including Agriculturists, Livestock Specialists, Agro-Industry Experts, Value Chain Analysts, Irrigation and Water Resource Engineers, Civil Engineers, Architects, Economists, Climate Resilience Specialists, M&E Experts, Procurement Professionals, ICT Experts, and Field Data Analysts. Most experts possess a minimum of a Master’s degree and over 10 years of experience delivering high-quality development solutions.

LKAK LIMITED has its head office in Abuja and provides services nationwide. The firm applies modern tools including KoboToolbox, STATA, GIS, satellite imagery and advanced MEL methodologies to support evidence-based planning and evaluation.

Past Experience & Track Record

LKAK LIMITED has played major roles in national development initiatives including:

  • Livestock Productivity & Resilience Support Project (LPRES)
  • Commercial Agriculture Development Project (CADP)
  • West Africa Agricultural Productivity Program (WAAPP)
  • APPEALS Project
  • Youth Employment & Social Support Operations (YESSO)
  • SLOGOR Governance Reform Project
  • SAPZ – Special Agro-Industrial Processing Zones
  • Food Systems Resilience Program

LKAK has carried out impact evaluations, baseline studies, feasibility assessments, safeguards integration, procurement support, and value chain advisory for multiple donor and government-funded development programs nationwide.

Selected Projects

LPRES Impact Evaluation

Consultancy for Impact Evaluation under the Livestock Productivity & Resilience Support Project.

LKAK Limited was awarded the contract by the French Development Agency (AFD) in collaboration with the Federal Ministry of Budget and National Planning. The assignment involved full impact evaluation delivery including baseline methodology, data collection, survey instruments, enumerator training, QA protocols, and policy briefs shaping nationwide livestock sector reforms.

SLOGOR Project Assessment

Sustainability assessment of governance reforms across six participating states.

Engaged by the National Project Coordination Unit of SLOGOR (Supported by the World Bank), LKAK Limited conducted a full sustainability review of governance reforms related to transparency, accountability, and long-term PFM systems. The assignment also included a comprehensive beneficiary assessment of MDAs and LGAs across six states.

Kaduna ICT Feasibility Study

Feasibility for ICT training expansion and digital inclusion framework.

Commissioned by the French Development Agency (AFD) and Kaduna State Government, LKAK conducted a feasibility study on the State ICT Implementation Plan. The study aligns with NIIEV and NEDS frameworks to scale ICT training for 1 million youths, reduce rural–urban digital divide, and promote e-learning and social inclusion.

APPEALS VCIP Analysis

Review and analysis of VCIPs for six participating states under APPEALS.

LKAK Limited reviewed 6-state Value Chain Implementation Plans under APPEALS for FMARD. The assignment evaluated plan feasibility, implementation gaps, recommendations for improved productivity, value addition, and stronger farmer–agribusiness linkages across priority commodity chains nationwide.
